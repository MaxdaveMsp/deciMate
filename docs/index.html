deciMate turns your iPhone into a practical SPL monitor with live dB readings, smart threshold alerts, and an expressive mascot that reacts to your sound levels.

Features

Everything you need
on stage or in the studio.

Built for rehearsals, DJ sets, small venues, and anyone who cares about sound levels.

🎚️

Live SPL Meter

A beautiful arc meter shows your current dB level in real time, with average and peak tracking across your session.

🤖

Expressive Mascot

A tiny audio-native mascot with white oval eyes reacts to your levels — calm when it's quiet, panicked at peak events.

⚡

Smart Thresholds

Set warning, critical, and peak thresholds. Choose from presets for classrooms, concerts, studios, and more — or go fully custom.

🎯

Calibration

Apply a manual offset using a trusted reference meter or acoustic calibrator to get practical readings for your device.

📊

Session Logging

Every session is recorded with a live chart and CSV export so you can review what happened after the show.

🔗

deciMate Live Link

Stream live SPL data as JSON over your local network to dashboards, scripts, or any HTTP-capable tool on the same Wi-Fi.

How it works

Enable Live Link in Settings. Any device on the same Wi-Fi can poll your iPhone for a live JSON snapshot:

{ "app": "deciMate", "timestamp": "2026-06-10T22:11:04Z", "spl_current": 87.6, "spl_average": 83.2, "spl_peak": 91.4, "threshold_state": "warning", "companion_state": "Getting loud..." }

Works with any HTTP client — browsers, Node.js, Python, Home Assistant, OBS, Shortcuts, and more.
