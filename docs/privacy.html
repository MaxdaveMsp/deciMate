Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

deciMate is built by David Torres / Loud and Beyond. This page explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and what leaves your device.

Microphone

deciMate requests access to your device microphone solely to measure sound pressure levels. Audio is processed in real time to calculate dB readings. No audio is ever recorded, stored, or transmitted. Raw audio samples are discarded immediately after the RMS value is computed.

Data stored on your device

The following data is stored locally on your iPhone only:

  • Calibration offset (a single number, saved in UserDefaults)
  • Threshold preferences — warning, critical, and peak dB values
  • Session samples during an active measurement session (held in memory, not persisted to disk unless you export)

None of this data is transmitted to any server.

Data you choose to export

You can export a session as a CSV file using the Export button. This file is created on demand and shared only through the iOS share sheet — you control where it goes. deciMate does not receive a copy.

deciMate Live Link (optional)

Live Link is an optional feature you must explicitly start. When active, it opens a local HTTP server on port 8080 that responds to requests from devices on the same Wi-Fi network with a JSON snapshot of the current SPL reading. This feature:

  • Only runs when you tap "Start Live Link"
  • Only responds to devices on your local network — it is not accessible over the internet
  • Does not store, log, or forward any data
  • Stops completely when you tap "Stop Live Link" or close the app

Analytics & tracking

deciMate does not use any analytics SDK, advertising framework, or tracking library. No data is collected about how you use the app.

Third-party services

deciMate uses no third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. All functionality is built with Apple's own frameworks (SwiftUI, AVFoundation, Combine, Network).

Children

deciMate does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no advertising or in-app purchases targeting children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
