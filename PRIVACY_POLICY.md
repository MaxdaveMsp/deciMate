# deciMate Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** June 10, 2026

## Who we are

deciMate is provided by Loud and Beyond / David Torres. For privacy or support questions, contact info@loudandbeyond.com.

## Summary

deciMate is designed as a practical sound-level monitoring tool that works locally on your device. We do not sell personal data, track users across apps or websites, or require an account.

## Information deciMate uses

- **Microphone access:** deciMate uses the iPhone microphone to estimate sound levels and display practical SPL readings. Audio is processed for measurement purposes.
- **Local session data:** deciMate may store sound-level readings, thresholds, calibration settings, and session history locally on your device so you can review or export your measurements.
- **Local network access:** if you enable deciMate Live Link, the app may make live measurement data available to other devices on the same local network.

## Audio recordings

deciMate is intended to analyze sound levels, not to record conversations or upload audio. The app does not send microphone audio to our servers.

## Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Session exports are user-controlled: if you export a CSV or share measurement data, that action is initiated by you.

## Analytics and tracking

The MVP version of deciMate is planned with no third-party advertising tracking and no account system. If analytics, crash reporting, subscriptions, or cloud features are added in the future, this policy will be updated before release.

## deciMate Live Link

Live Link is a local-network feature. When enabled, compatible clients on your network may read live SPL values and status information from your iPhone. Only enable Live Link on networks you trust.

## Children’s privacy

deciMate is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Your choices

- You can deny or revoke microphone permission in iOS Settings.
- You can delete local session history inside the app where supported, or remove the app from your device.
- You can keep Live Link disabled unless you need local-network streaming.

## Important measurement note

deciMate provides practical sound-level monitoring using the device microphone. It is not a certified Class 1 or Class 2 sound level meter and should not be used as the sole tool for legal, regulatory, or compliance measurements.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as deciMate evolves. Updates will be posted on the hosted privacy page with a revised effective date.
